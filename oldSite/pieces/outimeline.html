As part of an informational graphics class, I was given the prompt to create an infographic using a timeline. I decided to tell the story of Ohio University by combining a large horizontal timeline with graphic elements depicting the rolling hills of Athens, OH. The title, "First and Finest," comes from Ohio University being the first university in Ohio as well as the Northwest Territory. Individual elements were illustrated in Adobe Illustrator with textures added to different elements via Photoshop.
