The Cost of Commuting

infographic design, data visualization, illustration

Launch Full Infographic

An infographic which I created based off of a New York Times article on factoring commuting costs into the cost of living. The infographic was designed with the intention of being viewed on the web.

I played around representing statistics through illustrations of clouds, hot air balloons and other forms of transportation. I wanted to bring attention to how transportation costs can drastically increase or reduce the cost of living in an area. It's something that many people don't consider when searching for a place to live.
